HEALTH INSURANCE


Majority of rural people live below poverty line and they are not capable of affording the expenses on medical needs. Health insurance is a tough task for these people. The condition of illness not only deprived them from earning but also pushes them into deep debt.

Health insurance can play a crucial role in preventing people from burden of debts. Insurance can provide them support at the time of emergency and needs. The government has introduced many health insurance schemes for rural people during past years with affordable prices so that they can be covered by insurance without much burden on them.
